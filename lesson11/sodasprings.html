A Beginner’s Guide to Camping in Idaho

By Sara Sheehy

A night camped under Idaho’s starry skies is an experience that you won’t soon forget. From the north’s dense forests to the south’s open plains, camping in Idaho is a wonder, full of dramatic landscapes and expansive beauty.

In the past year, more and more people are embracing camping as a way to spend time with their families, enjoy the outdoors, and explore somewhere new. Idaho is a camper’s dream, offering everything from amenity-rich RV parks to backpacking routes that take you to the wildest corners of the state.
