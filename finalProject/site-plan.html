Temple Inn & Suites Site Plan

Site Purpose

There are currently 168 dedicated temples and many more to come. Wouldn't it be amazing to plan a trip to see them in a matter of a couple clicks? The Temple Inn & Suites website is dedicated to helping members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints visit temples with the convenience of staying a block a way. This site will provide temple information including closures and Covid 19 phase updates. Customers will also have access to weather updates for each temple they plan on visiting.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is directed towards adults and families who visit the Church of Jesus Christ of Latterday Saints Temples. This site makes booking and planning trips easy with the abundance of information that is included about the temples.
