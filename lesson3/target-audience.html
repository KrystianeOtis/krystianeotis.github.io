Target Audience

The audience that this website will be targeted towards will be outdoorsy people who are on the more spontaneous side. This includes individuals who enjoy fishing, fly fishing, camping, and hiking. These people will be able to refer to this site for daily and weekly weather reports. They will also have access to reports that involve nearby campsites and fish and game news. These website visitors will be prepared for their next adventure in our local nature.

Beth Boland

Married, mother of 4 kids, and always on the go. Beth Lives about an hour away from Preston, ID. Her husband and kids always have busy schedules, but they always make it a priority to make room for family time. They like to go camping one weekend a month in the summer when the kids are out of school. It is crucial that they know what to expect from the weather when going out of town.

Stan Hill

Born and Raised in Preston, ID. 32 years old and single. Stan owns his own business and is always on business calls or assisting his clients. Stan is an avid fisherman and considers himself as a pro. The only free time that he has to himself are his early morning fishing trips before work. It is very useful for Stan to be aware of the fishing conditions each morning and also enjoys reading about fishing tips from other local professionals.
